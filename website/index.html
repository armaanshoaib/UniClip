Real-time Sync

Built on Socket.io for sub-millisecond delivery. Copy text on one device, and it's instantly waiting on your other clipboard.

Native Integration

Android accessibility services and Windows clipboard APIs work seamlessly in the background without affecting performance.

Private Rooms

Your data stays completely isolated. Create secure, temporary rooms for your connected hardware and vanish when you disconnect.
